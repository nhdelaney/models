Command Edition Terrain

The first terrain I've painted. Pretty fun overall, and now I actually have nice-looking terrain to use in games.

Paints Used:

  • Walls: Rakarth Flesh, Nuln Oil all over, drybrush Rakarth Flesh, light drybrush Screaming Skull
  • Large Steel Surfaces: Chaos Black Primer, drybrush Leadbelcher and Stormhost Silver
  • Steel Details: Leadbelcher, Nuln Oil all over, drybrush Stormhost Silver
  • Red: Khorne Red, Nuln Oil all over, drybrush Evil Sunz Scarlet
  • Brass: Balthasar Gold, Nihilakh Oxide all over, drybrush Balthasar Gold
  • Screens: Kabalite Green, Sybarite Green reflection, Ardcoat
  • Keypads: Vallejo White, Flash Gitz Yellow
  • Dials: Vallejo White, Mephiston Red, Nuln Oil all over
  • Skulls: Zandri Dust, Seraphim Sepia all over, highlight Ushabti Bone and Screaming Skull
  • Caged Lights: Vallejo White, Nuln Oil all over
  • Red Lights: Mephiston Red, Ardcoat
  • Buttons: Vallejo White, either Skarsnik Green, Mephiston Red or Calgar Blue
  • Dents/Damage: Stormhost Silver applied with sponge brush
  • Cracked Edges: heavy drybrush Abaddon Black, Stormhost Silver applied with sponge brush
