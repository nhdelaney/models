Ork Boyz

A squad of Ork Boyz. Again, bought them to be sloppy and learn to paint, but ended up trying hard

Paints Used:

  • Green: Waaagh Flesh, Biel-tan Green all over, highlight Warboss Green and Skarsnik Green
  • Steel: Leadbelcher, Nuln Oil all over, edge hightlight Stormhost Silver
  • Brass: Runelord Brass, Nuln Oil all over, highlight Liberator Gold and Auric Armour Gold
  • Black: Abaddon Black, edge highlight Eshin Grey and Dawnstone
  • Leather: Dryad Bark, Agrax Earthshade all over, edge highlight Gorthor Brown
  • Bone/Teeth/Claws: Zandri Dust, Seraphim Sepia all over, highlight Ushabti Bone and Screaming Skull
  • Lenses: Corax White, Coelia Greenshade/Carroburg Crimson, second layer of shade along the bottom, dot of Corax White in the top, Arcdoat
  • Eyes: Mephiston Red, Ardcoat
  • Base: Stirland Mud, Agrax Earthshade, drybrush Ushabti Bone, Steel Legion Drab rim
