Veteran Guardsmen Kill Team

Death Korps of Krieg Guardsmen from Kill Team Octarius. Fun to paint all the different weapons for one squad.

Paints Used:

  • Red Coats: Khorne Red, Nuln Oil all over legs, Carrobrug Crimson all over coat, light drybrush of Evil Sunz Scarlet and Wild Rider Red
  • Steel: Leadbelcher, Nuln Oil all over, edge hightlight Stormhost Silver
  • Gold: Retributor Armour, Reikland Fleshshade all over, highlight Liberator Gold
  • Shiny Brass: Retributor Armour, Reikland Fleshshade all over, highlight Sycorax Bronze
  • Black: Abaddon Black, edge highlight Eshin Grey and Dawnstone
  • Backpacks: Zandri Dust, Seraphim Sepia all over, highlight Zandri Dust
  • Leather/Wood: Dryad Bark, Agrax Earthshade all over, edge highlight Gorthor Brown
  • Lenses/Scope: Vallejo White, Coelia Greenshade, Ardcoat
  • Gauges: Vallejo White, Mephiston Red
  • Plasma Pistol: Vallejo White, drybrush Ahriman Blue, light drybrush Vallejo White
  • Power Sword: Glaze Ahriman Blue all over, lightning Ahriman Blue, mix Ahriman Blue and Vallejo white up to pure white to highlight
  • Book: Zandri Dust pages, Dryad Bark cover and text
  • Flashlight: Vallejo White, glaze Flash Gitz Yellow, Ardcoat
  • Medkit bag: Celestra Grey, Nuln oil all over, Ulthuan Grey all over, highlight Vallejo White
  • Medkit supplies: Warboss Green, Mephiston Red, Macragge Blue
  • Map: Zandri Dust paper, Abaddon Black and Mephiston Red details
  • Flamer tank: Mephiston Red, Carroburg Crimson all over, highlight Evil Sunz Scarlet
  • Water jugs: Macragge Blue
  • Medals: Waaagh Flesh, Biel-tan Green all over, highlight Warboss Green. Macragge Blue, Nuln oil all over
  • Comm Handset: Zandri Dust receiver, Eshin Grey cord, highlight Dawnstone
  • Muddy Legs: Drybrush Dryad Bark
  • Base: Vallejo Brown Earth Texture, Dryad Bark, Agrax Earthshade all over, drybrush Screaming Skull, Steel Legion Drab rim
