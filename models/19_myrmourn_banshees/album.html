Myrmourn Banshees

These were a little harder than the Glaivewraith Stalkers because of all the white, but overall pretty fun with nice bases.

Paints Used:

  • Cloak: Vallejo White, Nihilakh Oxide, drybrush Vallejo White to blend
  • Body: Balthasar Gold, Nihilakh Oxide all over, highlight Balthasar Gold
  • Bone: Zandri Dust, Seraphim Sepia all over, highlight Ushabti Bone and Screaming Skull
  • Wood: Dryad Bark, Agrax Earthshade all over, highlight Gorthor Brown
  • Rusty Metal: Leadbelcher, Reikland Fleshshade all over, edge highlight Stormhost Silver
  • Bird: Abaddon Black, drybrush Eshin Grey and Dawnstone
  • Base Leaves: Waaagh Flesh, Biel-tan Green all over, highlight Warboss Green and Skarsnik Green
  • Base Roses: Mephiston Red, Carroburg Crimson all over, highlight Evil Sunz Scarlet
  • Base Gravel: Mechanicus Standard Grey, Agrax Earthshade all over
  • Base Flagstones: Mechanicus Standard Grey, Nuln Oil all over, edge highlight Dawnstone, Mechanicus Standard Grey rim
