Squig Mine

Little guy that comes with the Rukkatrukk Squigbuggy. Didn't want him getting lost or forgotten, so he got his own tiny base to make him more visible, plus he's just fun.

Paints Used:

  • Squig Skin: Mephiston Red, Carroburg Crimson all over, highlight Evil Sunz Scarlet and Wild Rider Red
  • Squig Eyes/Claws: Abaddon Black, Ardcoat
  • Base: Vallejo Brown Earth, Dryad Bark, Agrax Earthshade all over, drybrush Screaming Skull, Steel Legion Drab rim
