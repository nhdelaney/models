Blitz Bowl Humans

From the Blitz Bowl board game. Since it's a board game and the army won't be expanded, I maybe went a little overboard on the colour scheme, extra edge highlights and such.

Paints Used:

  • Red armour: Mephiston Red, Carroburg Crimson all over, thick glaze near edges Mephiston Red, edge highlight Mephiston Red, Wild Rider Red, Evil Sunz Scarlet
  • Red padding: Khorne Red, Carroburg Crimson all over, highlight Khorne Red
  • Skin: Cadian Fleshtone, Reikland Fleshshade all over, highlight Cadian Fleshtone and Kislev Flesh
  • Gold: Retributor Armour, Reikland Fleshshade all over, highlight Retributor Armour and Liberator Gold
  • Steel: Leadbelcher, Nuln Oil all over, highlight Stormhost Silver
  • Leather: Dryad Bark, Agrax Earthshade all over, highlight Gorthor Brown
  • Skulls: Zandri Dust, Seraphim Sepia all over, highlight Ushabti Bone and Screaming Skull
  • Grass: Waaagh Flesh, Biel-tan Green all over, highlight Warboss Green and Skarsnik Green
  • Ball: Mournfang Brown, Agrax Earthshade all over, highlight Skrag Brown
  • Base: Vallejo White Pumice just to cover slots and make a little texture, Mechanicus Standard Grey, Nuln Oil all over, highlight Dawnstone
  • Eagle Coin Logo: Khorne Red, Carroburg Crimson all over, highlight edges with a mix of Khorne Red and Mephiston Red, edge highlight Mephiston Red, Evil Sunz Scarlet, Wild Rider Red
