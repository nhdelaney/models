Rat Swarms

Custom Rat Swarms made from the scenery rats that come with other Skaven models. They have a lot more definition than the actual models, and I didn't want to put this many rats on the bases of other models.

Paints Used:

  • Dark Brown Fur: Dryad Bark, Agrax Earthshade all over, highlight Gorthor Brown
  • Light Brown Fur: Mournfang Brown, Agrax Earthshade all over, highlight Skrag Brown
  • Skin: Cadian Fleshtone, Agrax Earthshade all over, highlight Cadian Fleshtone, Glaze pustules with Flash Gitz Yellow
  • Bones: Zandri Dust, Agrax Earthshade all over, highlight Ushabti Bone and Screaming Skull
  • Rusty Metal: Leadbelcher, Agrax Earthshade all over, Typhus Corrosion for rusty areas, drybrush rust with Trollslayer Orange, highlight Stormhost Silver
  • Bronze: Balthasar Gold, Agrax Earthshade all over, highlight Sycorax Bronze, Nihilakh Oxide in recesses
  • Wrappings: Rakarth Flesh, Agrax Earthshade all over, highlight Pallid Wych Flesh
  • Red Eye Lenses: Mephiston Red, Agrax Earthshade all over, highlight Mephiston Red, Ardcoat
  • Base: Vallejo Brown Earth Texture, Dryad Bark, Agrax Earthshade all over, light drybrush Screaming Skull, Dryad Bark rim
