Gretchin

I got these guys so I could be fast and sloppy to learn to paint better, turns out I only take my time

Paints Used:

  • Green: Waaagh Flesh, Biel-tan Green all over, highlight Warboss Green and Skarsnik Green
  • Steel: Leadbelcher, Nuln Oil all over, edge hightlight Stormhost Silver
  • Brass: Runelord Brass, Nuln Oil all over, highlight Liberator Gold and Auric Armour Gold
  • Black: Abaddon Black, edge highlight Eshin Grey and Dawnstone
  • Leather: Dryad Bark, Agrax Earthshade all over, edge highlight Gorthor Brown
  • Teeth and Claws: Zandri Dust, Seraphim Sepia all over, highlight Ushabti Bone
  • Eyes: Mephiston Red, Ardcoat
  • Checkerboard Pattern: Corax White and Abaddon Black, Agrax Earthshade all over, highlight white with Corax White
  • Base: Stirland Mud, Agrax Earthshade, drybrush Ushabti Bone, Steel Legion Drab rim
  • Squig Skin: Mephiston Red, Carroburg Crimson all over, second layer of Carroburg Crimson on hair, highlight Evil Sunz Scarlet and Wild Rider Red
  • Squig Tongue: Screamer Pink, Druchii Violet all over, Ardcoat
  • Squig Eyes/Claws: Abaddon Black, Ardcoat
  • Teeth and Claws: Zandri Dust, Seraphim Sepia all over, highlight Ushabti Bone and Screaming Skull
