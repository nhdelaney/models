Sentinel

A fun walker to paint, but it took me forever to decide on a colour for the hull. Especially since I would want any future Imperial Guard models to be the same colour. I think it turned out well though, and I'm extremely proud of the driver's face and sunglasses.

Paints Used:

  • Hull: Incubi Darkness, Nuln oil in recesses, glaze Thunderhawk Blue near edges, edge highlight Thunderhawk Blue and Fenrisian Grey
  • Hull Rivets: Fenrisian Grey
  • Steel: Leadbelcher, Nuln Oil all over, highlight Stormhost Silver
  • White Details: Celestra Grey, Nuln Oil all over, highlight Ulthuan Grey and Vallejo White
  • Interior: Zandri Dust, Nuln Oil all over, highlight Zandri Dust and Ushabti Bone
  • Black: Abaddon Black, highlight Eshin Grey and Dawnstone
  • Pilot Uniform: Incubi Darkness, Nuln Oil all over, highlight Thunderhawk Blue and Fenrisian Grey, less precisely than vehicle
  • Pilot Face: Kislev Flesh, Reikland Fleshshade all over, highlight Kislev Flesh and Flayed One Flesh
  • Pilot Sunglasses: Kabalite Green, glaze Sybarite Green for gradient, thin line of Sybarite Green, Ardcoat
  • Screens/Lenses: Kabalite Green, highlight/detail Sybarite Green, Ardcoat
  • Red Lights: Mephiston Red, Nuln Oil all over, highlight Mephiston Red, Ardcoat
  • Interior Dials: Vallejo White, Nuln Oil
  • Lights: Vallejo White, very light glaze of Flash Gitz Yellow, Ardcoat
  • Flamethrower Tank: Mephiston Red, Nuln Oil all over, highlight Mephiston Red and Evil Sunz Scarlet
  • Flamethrower Nozzle: Balthasar Gold, Reikland Fleshshade all over, highlight Sycorax Bronze
