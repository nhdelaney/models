Skeleton Warriors

A few skeletons. Kept their armour and shields fairly clean instead of dirty as they would've been raised magically.

Paints Used:

  • Bone: Zandri Dust, Seraphim Sepia all over, drybrush Ushabti Bone and Screaming Skull
  • Metal: Leadbelcher, Nuln Oil all over, edge hightlight Stormhost Silver
  • Wood/Leather: Dryad Bark, Agrax Earthshade all over, edge highlight Gorthor Brown
  • Fabric: Mephiston Red, Carroburg Crimson all over, highlight Mephiston Red
  • Horn: Retributor Armour, Reikland Fleshshade, highlight Liberator Gold
  • Helmet Gem: Vallejo White, Druchii Violet all over, Ardcoat
  • Base: Vallejo Brown Earth, Agrax Earthshade, drybrush Screaming Skull, Steel Legion Drab rim, Gamers Grass TinyTufts Beige
